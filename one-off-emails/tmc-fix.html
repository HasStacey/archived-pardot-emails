Did You Hear? The New
TUNE® Marketing Console is here!

  

 


Hopefully you’ve already heard we launched the TUNE® Marketing Console - the new mission control for creating and optimizing your omnichannel mobile marketing strategy.

Some of the benefits of the TMC are:

  • Better results through unification of data from advertising attribution and app store analytics (and soon cross promotion)
  • Fast and beautiful reporting to drive actionable marketing insight
  • Easy to setup and use, saving you time and making you more money
  • Data and privacy practices you can trust
What can you do with your TUNE® Marketing Console (TMC)? Here are just a few of the highlights.

One Dashboard - Multiple Products &
Lots of Apps


With the unified dashboard, you have access to an entire suite of products all dedicated to empowering your marketing success.

Switching Between Products:
Click on the Switcher icon in the upper right-hand corner and select either MobileAppTracking or MobileDevHQ. Once you've launched either product, you can always navigate back to your Dashboard using the Switcher icon and selecting Dashboard.



Switching Between Mobile App Dashboards:
Click on the Switcher icon in the upper right-hand corner and select your mobile app from the RECENT APPS list. Once you've clicked on a different app, your Dashboard will re-load with that app's statistics. You can also search for your mobile app if you don't readily see it in the RECENT APPS list.


 

  

Customizable Omni-Channel KPIs


There are currently 10 different KPI cards (more on the way) for you to choose from based on your marketing strategy and goals. Pick one or pick them all, it's entirely up to you to customize your dashboard as you see fit.

You can learn more about each KPI card by reading TMC Card Previews. You can also learn more about customizing the dashboard layout, adding a card to the dashboard as well as removing a card.

  

Dig Deeper for a more Granular Analysis


So you've gotten an all up view of the KPIs that matter most to you. Now you want to dig deeper into a KPI and really see what's going on. Perhaps you're seeing a drop in installs for one of your advertising partners and obviously you want to see if your data will tell you why.

Simply click on the KPI title itself and you're driven straight into the corresponding report where you can view your data in much finer detail with the use of a range of filters and groupings.

Please keep in mind the following:

  • Drilling into a specific KPI card drives you into a specific report in either your MAT or MDHQ account.
  • Your TMC dashboard shows KPI cards on a per app basis. Once you drill down into a specific MAT or MDHQ report, the respective KPI will be shown across all of your apps in each account.

Ready to get under the hood and take the new TUNE® Marketing Console for a spin? Contact your account manager today and get access to your new mobile mission control.
